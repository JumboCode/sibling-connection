2018 Sibling Connections Gala

Buy Tickets

Friday, April 6th at 6:30 -10:30pm
The Center for Arts at the Armory

An entertaining evening of inspiration, philanthropy, and fun.

This promises to be a fun night at our most important fundraiser of the year -- helping Sibling Connections send kids to Camp to Belong, Sibling Sundays, Weekend Retreats, and our Youth Leadership programs.

Music by Boston's The Ward Eights will keep the crowd swinging and our Live & Silent Auctions will have something for everyone.

Doors open at 6:30 PM. Buy your ticket in advance online ($100) or at the door ($125). For additional ways to donate, please visit
www.siblingconnections.org/get-involved/donate/.

All proceeds directly support the kids we serve in our programs.

Parking

There is limited parking at the Armory. The Armory offers an overflow lot, which will be staffed with an attendant that will help direct guests. On-street 2-hour parking is available on Highland Avenue.

Additional parking is available at the below lots located directly across the street from the Armory:
Little Sisters of the Poor, 186 Highland Street, Somerville, MA
St. Catherine, 179 Summer Street, Somerville MA
(8-minute walk)

Interested in donating an item to our silent and live auction? We're currently soliciting a variety of items, so please reach out to us!
