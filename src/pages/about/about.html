About Us

Sibling Connections' mission is to support the creation of enduring family relationships and community connections for sisters and brothers separated by the foster care system in Massachusetts.

Our vision is for every child who is removed from their home and placed in the foster care system to maintain their sibling bond and be rooted in and surrounded by a community caring people invested in their health and well being.

A little more about what we do...

Sibling Connections is the only organization in Massachusetts designed to strengthen the sibling bond of children separated by foster care. While the sibling relationship is the longest, and one of the most important, relationships most people have in their lives, this is especially true for children who are removed from their families because of abuse or neglect.
