Our Programs

Sibling Connections offers two main programs for reuniting siblings who have been separated by their placement in foster care. Held every August, Camp To Belong Massachusetts is our week-long summer camp and our annual flagship event. Year round, we also offer monthly one-day reunion opportunities throughout Massachusetts via our Sibling Sundays programs. For information on the dates of any of these events, please see our calendar. If you have any questions about any of our programs, please contact us and we will get back to you shortly.
